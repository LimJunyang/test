Noel Gallagher's Guitars

1. Gibson Les Paul Standard

Noel is often associated with the Gibson Les Paul Standard, which he used extensively during the early days of Oasis. This guitar is known for its rich, warm tone and sustain, making it perfect for rock music.

2. Fender Stratocaster

Another iconic guitar in Noel's collection is the Fender Stratocaster. He has used various models over the years, favoring its versatility and bright sound, especially in songs like "Wonderwall."

3. Gibson J-200 Acoustic

Noel frequently uses the Gibson J-200 acoustic guitar for its beautiful sound and aesthetic appeal. This guitar features a large body, which contributes to its loud, resonant tone, ideal for strumming chords.

4. Epiphone Riviera

The Epiphone Riviera is another guitar Noel has been known to play. This semi-hollow body guitar offers a unique tone and is often used for specific songs that require a more jangly sound.
