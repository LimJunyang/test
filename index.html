Oasis

About the band

Oasis was a British rock band formed in Manchester in 1991. The group was originally composed of lead vocalist Liam Gallagher, guitarist and main songwriter Noel Gallagher, bassist Paul McGuigan, and drummer Tony McCarroll. The band became one of the leading figures in the Britpop movement of the 1990s.
